How SmartShield Works

A multi-layered approach to phishing protection

URL Analysis

Every URL is analyzed using advanced heuristics including domain reputation, typosquatting detection, and suspicious patterns.

Content Scanning

Page content is analyzed for phishing indicators including urgent language, suspicious attachments, and brand impersonation.

User Protection
